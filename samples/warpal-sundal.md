```
nevendil ber
  dellodil oma brender ber
  wintedil ringder ber
  bellodil ber
dinarry bandabillo sender bower
dellobillo way warpal sundal humballo
```

Translation:

```
Dream with me
  sink into my skin
  mingle with my breath
  awaken with me
While the sunlight shimmers in our quiet place
We sleep like the dew-deep earth
```
